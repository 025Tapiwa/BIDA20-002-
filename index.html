London Eye

Enjoy amazing 360-degree views over London from the London Eye, a rotating observation wheel which is 135 metres (443 ft) high. Spot some of the capital's most iconic landmarks, including Big Ben, the Houses of Parliament and Buckingham Palace.

Bus Tours

Choose from sightseeing tours of top London landmarks, night-time tours, movie tours and even tours with afternoon tea. Many bus tours let you jump on and off so you can visit attractions, have lunch or go shopping. Book your bus tour now!

London Museums

No visit to London is complete without checking out its fantastic selection of museums and galleries, from big hitters to quirkier specialist museums. You'll see there's one to suit every interest and many of them are free to visit.
